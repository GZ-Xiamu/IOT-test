What is this about


This is a Web Application for your IOT Pet Feeder.

You can monitor every sensor result of your pet feeder in here by clicking the diffrent sub section on the above.

Beside monitoring, you also can to perfrom some control on your pet feeder such as:
  • setting timer for your pet feeder to dispense food on the time you set
  • setting the amount of the food you would like to dispense for your pet
  • and also able to open and close the lid of food dispenser to drop out some food for your pet
